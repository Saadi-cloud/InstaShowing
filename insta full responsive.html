Something so simple shouldn't be so hard

Instashowing was founded by a college student who watched his family's real estate agent spend hours to schedule a few showing appointments. He knew there had to be a way to consolidate the inefficiencies of texting/calling other agents, MLS print-outs and scheduling changes.
